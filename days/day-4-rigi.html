Getting There

Recommended Route

Via Vitznau (Most Scenic)

Lucerne → Boat (60 min) → Vitznau → Cogwheel (30 min) → Rigi Kaltbad

~1.5 hours total. Your hotel guest card gives 50% off the Rigi trains.

Bad Weather Alternative

Via Arth-Goldau (All Rail)

Lucerne → Train (30 min) → Arth-Goldau → Cogwheel (45 min) → Rigi Kaltbad

~1.5 hours total. Use this if weather makes the boat unpleasant.

Today's Schedule

7:00 – 10:30 AM

Breakfast at Lucerne Hotel

Last meal here. Maybe a final stroll through Old Town if you have time.

11:00 AM – 12:00 PM

Check Out & Head to Pier

Lucerne checkout is at noon. Head to Pier 1 for the boat to Vitznau.

~12:00 – 1:30 PM

Journey to Rigi

Enjoy the boat ride across Lake Lucerne, then the cogwheel train up the mountain. The views are incredible.

~1:30 – 2:00 PM

Arrive Rigi Kaltbad

Drop bags at the hotel. Remember: spa access starts at 10 AM, so you could've used it earlier if you'd arrived by then!

2:30 – 6:00 PM

Mineralbad & Spa

The highlight of the day. 35°C mineral water, indoor pool flowing to outdoor infinity pool, sauna, steam bath. Designed by Mario Botta with stunning mountain views.

6:30 – 9:00 PM

Dinner at Hotel

The hotel restaurant is excellent and there aren't many other options on the mountain. Book a table with a view.

Spa Tips

  • Slippers: Not provided. Buy at reception (CHF 6.50) or bring your own
  • Robes: Provided at reception
  • Hours: 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM
  • Outdoor pool: Don't miss it—swimming in warm water with mountain views, even in January
  • Bring: Swimsuit, maybe a book for the relaxation area
☁️

Above the Fog

Rigi is famous for being above the fog line. Even if Lucerne is gray, you might emerge into brilliant sunshine at 1,450m. The spa works in any weather—fog can make it even more mystical.
