Cultural Notes

Swiss Customs

  • Punctuality: Extremely valued. Be on time for everything.
  • Volume: Swiss speak quietly in public. Americans are often perceived as loud.
  • Personal space: More reserved than Americans. Don't strike up conversations with strangers.
  • Quiet hours: 10pm-7am. Sunday noon is "sacred quiet time."
  • Recycling: Taken very seriously. Ask if unsure.
